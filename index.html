About Me

Hello! I'm Nathan, 22 years old and a student at James Madison Univeristy in Virginia.

I was never the best at academics in high school, and I found myself at the end of my senior
year without a college, a plan, or much drive to make anything of myself. I began attending
community college in Northern Virginia as a way to gain a path forward. But I found much more
than that. I found a love of learning, a natural curiosity, and a desire to begin my academic
journey in earnest as soon as I could.

I began at James Madison Univeristy a year and a half later, as a student of Computer Science.
Since then, I have found an insatiable appetite for the world of computing, and recently,
mathematics. I've learned things I never could have imagined on my first day in Harrisonburg,
found new hobbies, and met many interesting and brilliant people. This is my final semester at
JMU, and while I will miss my school dearly, I cannot wait to see what the world has in store for
me next.

Go dukes!
